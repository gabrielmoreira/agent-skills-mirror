# Response Templates

Standard language for investor audit responses. Customize with your company details.

## Type A: Investment Confirmation (Simple)

```
Dear [AUDITOR_NAME],

Thank you for reaching out regarding [INVESTOR_FUND]'s investment in [YOUR_COMPANY_NAME].

We confirm the following as of [DATE]:

INVESTMENT POSITION:
- Shares: [X] Series [Y] Preferred Stock
- Price per share: $[X.XX]
- Total investment: $[X,XXX,XXX]
- Ownership: [X.XX]% undiluted / [X.XX]% fully diluted

[INVESTOR_FUND] holds no loans, convertible notes, or other instruments.

Please find attached our cap table as of [DATE].

For questions, please contact me at [YOUR_EMAIL].

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
[YOUR_TITLE], [YOUR_COMPANY]
```
